3D Art

While I was doing my Game Design certificate, I began to dive into the 3D art side of game design. I learnt how to use many different industry level programs such as, Maya, Arnold, Substance 3D and ZBrush. I learnt the basics of many different sides of this expansive industry such as, 3D modelling, 3D sculpting, 3D animation, rendering, texture painting and texture creation. Learning about this exciting world of art was one of the most rewarding things I have done in my life. Learning things I love through a school environment was the reason I had the courage to start the Bachelor of IT with RMIT.
